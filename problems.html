Problems counselling can help with

Counselling can help people with a wide range of issues, for example:
  • difficulties with personal or professional relationships
  • emotional distress: anxiety, depression, grief, stress, anger
  • eating disorders, panic attacks, self harm
  • bereavement, job loss, divorce
  • coping with change and crises
  • traumatic events
  • experience of bullying, abuse, violence
  • making important decisions / choices
  • self esteem, confidence or identity issues
  • dissatisfaction or lack of direction in life
